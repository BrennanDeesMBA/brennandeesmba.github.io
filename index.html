About

Howdy, I’m Brennan Dees, a dual master’s candidate at Texas A&M University, pursuing an MBA with a finance focus and a Master of Land & Property Development. I’m building a career in commercial real estate development and real estate finance, with a particular interest in combining investment decisions, capital strategy, and project execution from early planning through delivery.

I bring a mix of finance and real‑world development experience: I’m comfortable building pro formas, structuring capital stacks, and working through cash flow projections, but I also understand how projects actually get built and where they can run into friction in the field, schedule, entitlement process, or budget. That combination has helped me contribute to complex projects such as the Ritz‑Carlton Condos in The Woodlands, a 58‑story high‑rise in Austin, and a mass timber office building in Bridgeland, where I’ve supported underwriting, cash flow modeling, and capital structure decisions.

I’m especially proud of leading the finance work for an $828 million mixed‑use plan in the ULI Hines Competition, where I pulled together market assumptions, risk factors, phasing, and capital structure into a clear narrative an investor could follow. Beyond project work, I’ve led a team of 16 undergraduate resident advisors, which sharpened my communication, conflict‑management, and leadership skills in high‑responsibility settings. I’m motivated by opportunities where I can underwrite with discipline, communicate insights clearly, and help turn strong ideas into bankable plans that perform in practice.

Joining KKR

I’m excited to share that I’ll be joining KKR on their K-Star team as a Direct Lending Analyst in Dallas, TX, starting July 2026. I’m looking forward to working on deals across the United States and across various asset classes, learning from leading professionals in the industry, and diving into the private credit world.
